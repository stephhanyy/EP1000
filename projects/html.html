our task was to recreate this website with our own image and information

since i already knew how to do this, i completed it before class. however, turns out the way i did this was different from the way it was went through in class. to achieve the spacing and alignment of the useful information next to the image, i used css to give it a left float and margin size. how it was taught in class was using a table with <table> and <td> to place them side by side. tables were actually not taught in design school, perhaps because we don't deal with data much.
